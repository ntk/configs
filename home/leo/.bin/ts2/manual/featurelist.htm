Feature list

 

Some (not all) features of TeamSpeak 2

•Multiple codecs ranging from low bandwidth consumption to GSM sound quality

•Channel creation

•Webadmin access to server

•Passworded servers, channels

•Completely configurable keybindings (Windows client only at the moment)

•Auto voice normalizing (no more adjusting your mic manually)

•Supports Gamevoice hardware

•Windows/Linux server

•Windows/Linux client

•Event driven voice announcements (which can be configured to your taste)

•Text messaging in the client (for those complicated URL’s or I.P.’s)

•Whisper functions so you can talk to inidividuals, groups of persons or other channels without other people hearing you.

•IRC based admin functions for your server (like server admin, channel admin, operators)
