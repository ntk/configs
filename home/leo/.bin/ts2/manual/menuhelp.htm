Menu Help

 

In this menu you can find out something about the version you are currently using, who are on TeamSpeak Team and credits for the components. If you are reading this you probably came here via this menu.

If you still have questions about the workings of the client please visit the TeamSpeak forum and post a help request. If your question is unique ( not posted before, that’s why we have a Search function on the forum and a FAQ section) you will most likely get an answer by enthousiastic TeamSpeak supporters. If your question isn’t answered there you can visit the IRC channel of TeamSpeak at quakenet at #teamspeak.
