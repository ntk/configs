Request Voice

 

When you are on a moderated server or channel and you are not given Voice status (so you cannot speak to others, only listen) you can request Voice. When you click on it you can give a reason why you want voice or why admins should give you Voice. When you click on Ok a spoken message (Voice Requested) will be given on the server and behind your name you will acquire the WV flag (Wants Voice) and the reason you entered.
